## Why SERP LinkedIn DL

LinkedIn has no native download button for feed videos or images. Videos use streaming CDN delivery that prevents right-click saving, and post text is buried in complex page structures that break on copy-paste. Generic download tools do not understand LinkedIn's feed layout. SERP LinkedIn DL is purpose-built for LinkedIn and handles all of this automatically.

## Key features

- Scans LinkedIn feed and post pages for all downloadable content
- Detects video streams from HTML5 elements, embedded metadata, and CDN requests
- Smart image filtering captures post images while excluding profile photos, logos, and icons
- Saves post text as clean text files with the original post URL and timestamp
- Bulk download saves all visible content with one click
- Auto-organizes files into LinkedIn Downloader/{author}/ folders with dates in filenames

## How it works

1. **Install** the extension in your browser
2. **Browse** to your LinkedIn feed, a post page, or a video page
3. **Scan** by clicking the extension icon to detect all visible content
4. **Download** individual items or use bulk download to save everything at once

## Supported formats

- **Images:** JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF in their original format and resolution
- **Text:** TXT files with post content, original URL, and timestamp metadata

## FAQ

**How do I download a LinkedIn video?**
Go to any LinkedIn page with video content, click the extension icon, and download from the detected results.

Videos (MP4), images (original format), and post text (TXT files with metadata).

**Does this capture every image on the page?**
No. Smart filtering excludes profile photos, logos, icons, and UI elements.

**Where are my downloads saved?**
To a LinkedIn Downloader folder in your Downloads directory, organized by author name.
